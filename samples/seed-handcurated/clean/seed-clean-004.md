# Dal Tadka

A simple yellow-lentil dish, ready in about thirty minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup yellow lentils (toor dal), rinsed
- 3 cups water
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp ghee or oil
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small tomato, diced
- 1 green chili, slit (optional)
- Fresh cilantro, chopped, to garnish

## Method

1. Rinse the dal until the water runs mostly clear.
2. Pressure-cook with the water, turmeric, and salt for four whistles, then let the pressure release naturally.
3. While the dal cooks, heat ghee in a small pan. Add cumin seeds; let them sputter for a few seconds.
4. Add the onion, garlic, and chili. Cook on medium until the onion is soft and just starting to brown.
5. Add the tomato. Cook until it breaks down and the oil begins to separate.
6. Pour the tempering over the cooked dal. Stir gently. Simmer for two minutes to combine.
7. Garnish with cilantro.

Serve hot with rice or roti.
